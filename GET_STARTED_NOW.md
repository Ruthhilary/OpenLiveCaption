**Best for**: Quick sharing with friends

**Steps**:
1. Zip your OpenLiveCaption folder
2. Upload to Google Drive/Dropbox
3. Set sharing to "Anyone with link"
4. Share the link

**Time**: 2 minutes

**Best for**: Non-technical users (no Python needed)

**Steps**:

2. Wait 10-20 minutes
3. Upload `dist/OpenLiveCaption.exe` to cloud storage
4. Share download link

**Time**: 30 minutes (mostly waiting)

**Best for**: Browser-based access

**Steps**:
1. Sign up at render.com or railway.app
2. Connect GitHub repository
3. Deploy
4. Share your URL

**Time**: 15 minutes
